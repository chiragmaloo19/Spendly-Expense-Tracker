# 💸 Spendly — Your Personal Finance Tracker

Spendly is a simple yet powerful web app built to help you stay on top of your money without the stress. It’s designed to be fast, clean, and visually appealing, so you can quickly understand where your money is going and make better financial decisions.

## 🚀 What it’s about

Managing finances shouldn’t feel complicated or boring. Spendly gives you a smooth, modern interface where you can instantly see your financial status and also dive deeper into your spending habits when needed. The best part? It runs entirely in your browser—no setup, no backend, no hassle.

## ✨ What you can do

* **Track everything easily**
  Add your income and expenses with categories, dates, and notes—quick and simple.

* **Get a clear overview**

  * See your **current balance** instantly
  * Check your **monthly spending**
  * Spot your **biggest expense**
  * Know how active you’ve been with your **transaction count**

* **Understand your spending visually**

  * A **doughnut chart** shows where your money goes
  * A **bar chart** compares income vs expenses over the last 6 months

* **Stay in control**

  * Filter transactions by type or category
  * Export all your data as a clean **CSV file** anytime

* **Your data stays yours**
  Everything is stored in your browser using LocalStorage—no sign-ups, no servers.

* **Looks good everywhere**
  A responsive, modern design that works smoothly on desktops and tablets.

## 🛠️ Built with

* Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
* HTML5 & modern CSS (Flexbox, Grid)
* Chart.js for charts
* Google Fonts (Inter)
* Simple icons using emojis & Unicode

## 📊 How it’s laid out

* **Left side**: Add transactions and view your history
* **Right side**: Interactive dashboard with charts that update instantly
* **Notifications**: Small toast messages for actions like adding, deleting, or exporting
